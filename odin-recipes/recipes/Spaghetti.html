Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Simple yet incredibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy, then add garlic.
  4. Toss spaghetti in the pan with pancetta, then remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately.
