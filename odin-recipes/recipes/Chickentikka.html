Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

A rich and creamy Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until golden brown, then set aside.
  3. Melt butter in the same pan, then add tomato puree and simmer.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and cooked chicken.
  5. Simmer for another 10 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
