Juicy Roasted Chicken

Description

This recipe is a delightful and flavorful dish that is perfect for any occasion. With a blend of fresh ingredients and aromatic spices, it is both easy to prepare and sure to impress.

Whether you are cooking for family or guests, this dish is a great way to bring people together and enjoy a homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ingredient one
  • 2 tbsp ingredient two
  • 1/2 tsp ingredient three
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Place the mixture into a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
